We offer a range of training packages

Group Outdoor Class

Length: 1 hour 30 minutes

Group lesson with 4-5 puppies or adults. A group class includes teahcing basic obidence as well as some handouts addressing basic manners for your dog. Homework will be given and a quick follow-up will take place via email or phonecall 2 weeks after the class

One to One Class

Length: 2 hours

Need help with your dog in a park, at the vet, or somewhere else away from home? This private lessons will help with that. Melanie will meet you at the location and spend time with you and your dog working on overcoming any obstacles that may be thrown your way.Homework will be given and a quick follow-up will take place via email or phonecall 2 weeks after the class

Home Visit

Length: Varies

A private in-home lessons can be done in your home, yard and/or local area, and take place with you and your family. These lessons are ideal for follow-up training from Group Lessons as well as fine tuning or focusing on some more significant challenges and problem behaviours. Homework will be given and a quick follow-up will take place via email or phonecall 2 weeks after the class
